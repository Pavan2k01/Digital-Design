Programme structure

The Chandrayaan (Indian Lunar Exploration Programme) programme is a multiple mission programme.

Orbiter and Impactor

Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee announced the Chandrayaan project on course in his Independence Day speech on 15 August 2003. The mission was a major boost to India's space program.

Soft Landers and Rovers

On 18 September 2008, the First Manmohan Singh Cabinet approved the mission Chandrayan-2. In November 2019, ISRO officials stated that a new lunar lander mission was being studied for launch in November 2020. This new proposal is called Chandrayaan-3
